ABOUT

Welcome to the official portfolio of Lightning Horn Unicorn, formerly known as Vedran Lukic.

Now, grab a coffee and hop into the Fur-rarri.

What is a Fur-rarri? It’s a unique vehicle covered in fur, making even the hairiest celebrities look bald. I have fur, I have a car, Uh! Fashion!

Join us on an exciting journey through Vienna, Austria (affectionately known as Schnitzelland), where the creations of this visionary artist resonate with the impact of a memorable musical performance.

A Musical Journey

It’s 2002: Picture a young Vedran with a borrowed electric guitar and a half-broken, duct-taped laptop running a demo version of FL Studio. His neighbors wonder if he's building a time machine.

Despite these humble beginnings, his passion never wavered, transforming his career into something remarkable. "Music was my escape and my expression," he recalls. "I remember stumbling upon my first creations and thinking, 'Hey, that’s definitely something rare cooking here. Or maybe it’s just the CPU bench-pressing those MIDI files.'"

Innovative Sound and Lyricism

Lightning Horn Unicorn transcends traditional boundaries, masterfully composing across various Digital Audio Workstations (DAWs). His blend of personal touch and cutting-edge innovation, including artificial intelligence, pushes the limits of musical expression. Think of him as the Da Vinci of DAWs, but with fewer paintbrushes and more keyboards.

The essence of Lightning Horn Unicorn's artistry lies in his profound and vivid lyricism. His songs are a tapestry of intricate imagery and deep themes, blending personal experiences with universal truths.

“Every song is a story, every lyric a piece of my thoughts,” he shares. Music is a reflection of the world. And sometimes, that world is just weird. Especially the internet, and we all know that. But you can’t just throw a guitar into an interdimensional wormhole and expect a Grammy. So he works on his stuff to make it better (input flying dragons blowing flames with guitar synthesizers around their backs here).

Themes of awakening and realization are prevalent in his work, with haunting melodies and striking imagery symbolizing moments of clarity. Lightning Horn Unicorn’s exploration of the intersection between technology and human connection uses witty tech references to describe emotional intimacy. It’s like tech support, but with feelings.

Overcoming darkness and adversity is a powerful theme in his music, with epic backdrops of mystical battles and personal triumphs creating an engaging and inspirational experience.

Additionally, he celebrates cultural preservation through heroic imagery and communal rituals, bridging the old and the new and adding layers of depth to his work. Because who doesn’t love a good heroic anthem about saving coffee culture? We drink and love coffee every day.

Achievements and Aspirations

From 2012 to 2016, Lightning Horn Unicorn built a successful home music studio in Carinthia, Austria, connecting with over 3000 supporters. “Every supporter was a beacon of hope, a testament to the power of music,” he reflects. Or, in less poetic terms, “You guys rock!”

Join the Journey

If Lightning Horn Unicorn's story resonates with you, he welcomes your support. Explore his art and music, and join him on this exciting journey of self-expression and innovation. "Together, we can create something extraordinary," he invites. Or at least, we’ll have a lot of fun trying.
