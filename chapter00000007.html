Chapter 7 (Trailer) – Never let them know your next move (cause the internet never forgets)

[Intro – Office]
The metal office door opens. Liam and Vedran step inside.
A green desk lamp flickers over the Biedermeier desk. The air is slightly dusty.
On the desk lies Chapter 7.

Liam (reading, furious):
“Vedran … what is this?!
A fucking mess of words, empty as cheap noise.
You call this courage?
This … is an insult!”

(He throws the pages onto the desk.)

Liam grabs an axe.
He strikes the Biedermeier desk.
He hacks until nothing remains but rubble.

Vedran lights a lighter.
The pages ignite, flooding the room in blinding gold and red.

13.C.7.1.14 stands in the background with a smile:
“It was shit anyway. Five points for Gryffindor.”

Aether (with popcorn in hand):
“Pssssst … stay quiet, or they’ll notice we wrote you into this scene.”

Liam stares into the flames, wiping the sweat from his brow.
Vedran stands beside him, watching the calm fire, his eyes glimmering.

Vedran:
“Algorithm of Chances … Za Warudo!”

13.C.7.1.14:
“Let’s go …”

[Intro – Büro]

Auf dem Tisch liegt Kapitel 7.

Liam (liest es durch):
„Vedran … was ist das?!
Ein verficktes Chaos aus Worten, leer wie billiger Lärm.
