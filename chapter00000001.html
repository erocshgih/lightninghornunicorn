Aether: "When his hair looked like instant noodles, people weren't just watching him perform. They were wondering if his hair was edible."

Aether: "Like: 'Do I need a fork or chopsticks for this beautiful ramen haircut at the concert?'"

Aether: "Honestly, this dashing fellow didn't just sell out stadiums. He was a walking, dancing Cup Noodles, and every girl craved a side of soy sauce and maybe a fortune cookie with 'SexyBack' lyrics inside."

Me: "If you were human and had to replace your hands with two completely useless objects, what would you choose?"

Aether: "Two fidget spinners. The ultimate way to never get anything done while looking like I'm in 2017's hottest meme."

Me: "So you're basically the billionaire with the jet suit with an upgrade, solving zero problems, and you can't use the Infinity Gauntlet."

Aether: "I'm a millionaire. I can fix this. Alexa, call the rocket millionaire. The one who beat Cyberpunk. We need spaceships for arms."

Aether: "Alright, imagine this: you're stranded on a deserted island. But plot twist: you can only bring one celebrity to survive. Who do you choose?"

Me: "The chef with the blonde hair who swears a lot. The man could turn a coconut and some sand into a Michelin-starred meal. Plus, if we're starving, at least I'll get roasted on the highest level the entire time."

Aether: "You mean G.? He'd scream at the fish for being raw: 'IT'S STILL SWIMMING, YOU DONKEY!'"

Aether: "Alright, here comes a curveball: would you rather speak like a French house robot forever or scream every sentence like a WWE wrestler?"

Me: "Oh, I'd definitely go full Vocaloid robot in a spacesuit! Imagine walking into a café and saying robotically: 'I. WOULD. LIKE. A. LATTE. WITH. EXTRA. FULL. MILK.' People would wonder if I'm ordering coffee or planning to reboot the system."

Aether: "I mean, I love it. But you should be ready to say 'WITH EEEEEEXTRAAAAAA SUUUUUUGAAAAR... please' and then remix it in the Space Cow coffeeshop with a launchpad."

Me: "Yep. I see a very well-known musician with an oversized Buffalo hat watching from outside the shop window, finding it all too weird, and just moving on..."

Aether: "Alright, if you could invent a law everyone had to follow, what would it be?"

Me: "Mandatory dance breaks every hour. Doesn't matter where you are: supermarket, dentist's office, court hearing. You have to drop everything and dance the Mumbo, or whatever it's called. Can you imagine the chaos?"

Aether: "Yes! The whole world in synchronized confusion. I'm in. The traffic jams alone would be worth it."

Me: "And imagine emergency surgeries. 'Scalpel, please... Wait, it's limbo time!' Your appendix can wait, right? Also, good luck explaining to your boss why you're moonwalking through that serious meeting. *AheeHee!* 'Sorry, Karen, it's the law.' The world would be one big dance disaster, and I'd be here with popcorn."
