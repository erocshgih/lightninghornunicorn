About Me

Welcome to my YouTube channel! I'm Vedran, a passionate songwriter based in Vienna, Austria. Here, I pour my heart and soul into crafting melodies and lyrics that resonate with me.

While I love strumming my acoustic guitar and exploring visual art, my musical journey has evolved to include digital production. Since 2005, I've been hooked on FL Studio, using it to create tracks that blend my personal touch with modern innovation.

In recent years, I've been experimenting with artificial intelligence to push my creativity even further. By combining traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology, I'm constantly seeking new ways to express myself through music.

Join me on this journey of self-expression through music.
