Lightning Horn Unicorn

Welcome to the official portfolio of Lightning Horn Unicorn, formerly known as Vedran Lukic. Now, grab a coffee and hop into the Fur-rarri for a wild ride through Vienna, Austria (Schnitzelland), where this visionary artist’s tunes hit harder than a wet Banana Joe neck-slap.

A Musical Journey

It’s 2002: Picture a young Vedran with a borrowed electric guitar and a half-broken, duct-taped laptop running a demo version of FL Studio. His neighbors wonder if he's building a time machine. Despite these humble beginnings, his passion never wavered, transforming his career into something remarkable. "Music was my escape and my expression," he recalls. "I remember stumbling upon my first creations and thinking, 'Hey, that’s definitely something rare cooking here. Or maybe it’s just the CPU bench-pressing those MIDI files.'"

Innovative Sound and Lyricism

Lightning Horn Unicorn transcends traditional boundaries, masterfully composing across various Digital Audio Workstations (DAWs). His blend of personal touch and cutting-edge innovation, including artificial intelligence, pushes the limits of musical expression. Think of him as the Da Vinci of DAWs, but with fewer paintbrushes and more keyboards.

The essence of Lightning Horn Unicorn's artistry lies in his profound and vivid lyricism. His songs are a tapestry of intricate imagery and deep themes, blending personal experiences with universal truths.
