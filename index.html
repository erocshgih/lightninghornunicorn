About

Welcome to the official portfolio of Vedran, known in the music world as Lightning Horn Unicorn. Based in the heart of Vienna, Austria, Vedi is not just a songwriter; he is a visionary artist whose melodies and lyrics are meticulously crafted from the depths of his soul.

Lightning Horn Unicorn embodies unwavering dedication and extraordinary versatility. Since 2005, his musical journey began with the acoustic guitar, synthesizers, and drums, effortlessly merging his love for visual art into his compositions. Over the years, his sound has transcended traditional boundaries, embracing the digital revolution. Vedran masterfully composes music across diverse DAWs, crafting tracks that blend his personal touch with cutting-edge innovations.

In a groundbreaking move, Vedran has ventured into the avant-garde realm of artificial intelligence, integrating it into his creative process. This fusion of traditional craftsmanship with state-of-the-art technology pushes the boundaries of his music, exploring new ways to articulate his artistic vision.

Join Lightning Horn Unicorn on this exhilarating ride of self-expression and innovation. Step into a world where every note is a brushstroke, every melody a masterpiece, and every performance a journey into the extraordinary.
