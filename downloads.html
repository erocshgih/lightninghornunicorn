Thanks for supporting an independent artist from Vienna, Austria.

I've been creating music since 2008, driven by curiosity, storytelling, and a love for exploring different musical worlds. Whether it's an 80s synth anthem, an electronic journey, a heavy metal track, or a quiet acoustic piece, every song begins with an idea that I genuinely care about. At my core, I'm a songwriter. Creating music the traditional way, with a guitar, honest songwriting, and genuine human performances, is still what I enjoy most. It's how many of my songs begin, and it's the creative process I value above all else.

Over the years, however, I found it incredibly difficult to build a reliable band, find musicians who could commit long-term, or work with people who shared the same creative vision. Rather than leaving hundreds of ideas unfinished, I decided to embrace modern technology as another creative instrument.

Some of the songs on this website were created with the help of AI tools as part of that process. For me, AI doesn't replace creativity. It doesn't replace emotion, imagination, or the countless nights spent searching for exactly the right melody, atmosphere, arrangement, or sound. Just like a guitar, a synthesizer, or a recording studio, it's simply another tool that helps me bring ideas to life.
Stay kind. Stay curious. Stay inspired.
